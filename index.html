Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a cost to attend these webinars? +
All our webinars are completely free to attend as they are community-driven initiatives by Microsoft MVPs.
How often are webinars held? +
We aim to host webinars on the first week of each month. Times will vary so we can cover each timezone.
Can I submit questions before the webinar? +
Yes, we encourage attendees to submit questions during registration which helps our MVPs prepare better answers.
Are these webinars recorded? +
Yes, recordings are made available to registered attendees after the live session on the WinAdmins YouTube Channel.
Is this an official Microsoft event? +
No, these webinars are community-driven initiatives by Microsoft MVPs and are not official Microsoft events.
Can we sponsor the event? +
No.
Who is running this thing? +
Lewis Barry MVP with support from Johannes Geir Kristjansson MVP and the WinAdmins community.
What is the MVP host selection criteria? +
We aim to accept everyone. For the first few episodes, I've (Lewis) reached out to people I know, but I welcome (and want) any MVP who is interested to apply.
